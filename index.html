Torghast Rune Chest Puzzle

Instructions

Your goal is to unlock all 4 chains by finding the rune that opens each chain, which is determined at the beginning of each puzzle.

You can change the runes by clicking on the skulls. Keep in mind:

  • One skull will change all 4 runes.
  • Another skull will change 3 runes (not the rune above the skull that changes 4 runes).
  • Another skull will change 2 runes (not the runes above the skulls that change 4 and 3 runes).
  • The last skull will change 1 rune (just the rune above it).

First, you want to unlock the chain above the skull that changes 4 runes. Click on the skull until it is unlocked. Once it is unlocked, you won't need to click it again.

Then, do the same with the chain above the skull that changes 3 runes, then 2 runes and lastly 1 rune.
