Torghast Rune Chest Puzzle

Instructions

Unlock all 4 chains by finding the rune that opens each chain. Each rune will unlock only one chain.

You can change the runes by clicking on the skulls. Keep in mind:

  • One skull will change 4 runes.
  • Another skull will change 3 runes.
  • Another skull will change 2 runes.
  • The last skull will change 1 rune.

Hints

  • The skull that changes 3 runes will not change the rune above the skull that changes 4 runes.
  • The skull that changes 2 runes will not change the runes above the skulls that change 4 and 3 runes.
  • The last skull will change only the rune above itself.

Knowing this, you first want to unlock the chain above the skull that changes 4 runes. Click on the skull until it is unlocked. Once it is unlocked, you don't need to click it again.

Then, do the same with the chain above the skull that changes 3 runes, then 2 runes and lastly 1 rune. Enjoy your anima power!
