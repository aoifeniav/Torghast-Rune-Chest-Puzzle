Torghast Rune Chest Puzzle

Your goal is to unlock all 4 chains by finding the rune that opens each chain, which is determined at the beginning of the puzzle. Each rune will only unlock one chain.

You can change the runes by clicking on the skulls.

Keep in mind:

  • One skull will change 4 runes.
  • Another skull will change 3 runes.
  • Another skull will change 2 runes.
  • The last skull will change 1 rune.

The skulls that change 3, 2 and 1 runes won't change the rune above the skulls that change more runes. That is:

  • The skull that changes 3 runes won't change the rune above the skull that changes 4 runes.
  • The skull that changes 2 runes won't change the runes above the skulls that change 4 and 3 runes.
  • The last skull will just change the rune above itself.

Knowing this, you first want to unlock the chain above the skull that changes 4 runes. Click on the skull until it is unlocked. Once it is unlocked, you won't need to click it again.

Then, do the same with the chain above the skull that changes 3 runes, then 2 runes and lastly 1 rune. Enjoy your anima power!
